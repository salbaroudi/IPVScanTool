Standard Setup:

Materials:
- Use Currys Green Measuring Board
- Black foam card for boundary setting
-IPEVO camera plugged in.
- Two thorfire multicolored lamps.


1) Mat, lamps and camera are aligned on desk
2) Paper is placed in marked area on Curry's cutting board.
3) IPEVO chromium software is started, and fine adjustments are made if necessary.
	- camera is centered around the paper and markings
	 - maximal resolution is used.
	  - two lights are put on either side of the document, or directly on each side of the scanner base itself.
4) Scans are done, and saved to respective folder.

Note: See images in this folder to achieve camera positioning and zoom
